Family & Home Shopping Management system

ABOUT

The new system which is using the smart wrist
bracelet will make people’s life even much easier. By
using the smart wrist bracelet, you can easily
convert what you say into an electronic note and
you can add it to your daily plans. So, you can
simply make a list of things to do, including things
to do at home and things to buy outside for your
home. You can easily get in touch with your family
members through this mobile application. You can
get additional tasks or to-do lists from them and
even you are able to edit these tasks. Additionally,
there is a feature called System Deep Learning
which creates a list of daily things to do and
products to be purchased in terms of what you have
done and purchased so far daily.
The system are able to be operated by both wrist bracelet and mobile application independently.
